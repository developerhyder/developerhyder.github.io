A Man in his full potential:

History bears witness to human greatness. A small mutation in the consciousness pattern has led to a species which is so advanced , that it tames the very nature of life and death. A species with such power loves to play god in every aspect of existentialism .  A man in his potential has realised the fact that very existence is a thought. And every thought and emotion is a derivative  of the following three principles. The very first principle is the “survival of the self” this led to the genesis of fear, hunger and other bodily processes which are required for a man to survive. The second principle is the “survival of progeny” . This led to the genesis of care and affection to the progeny. Do you know why it irritates when you hear a baby cry, it is because our brain is hardwired to comply with the second principle. The third principle is the “survival of the species” it is due to this principle that we as humans started to live in groups and thus creating sects and communities. The idea of religion was evolved from the third principle to create peace in chaos. The entire universe works by creating peace out of chaos. From wave pattern in water when a stone is dropped in to it to human evolution. The idea of peace persists. The yin and the yang exists and thus creating a balance on which the entire world runs. This realisation has made man invincible to any sort of natural calamities and changes.

        Also the existence of individuals who had seeded thoughts into their minds , carefully nurtured them and patiently waited for the fruits has led to inventions, innovations and revolutions which has paved way for mankind achieve great things. Indeed , Man was not built weak nor was he mechanical in his approach towards problems and their solutions. Man in his ignorance and arrogance has signed contracts and agreements with the devil which hinder him from growth, from achieving great things and from doing things which he was designed to do. Man defies laws and agreements which were signed by our ancestors so that mankind can stay in accord with the above three principles. Furthermore, the congress of thoughts between mankind and his potential is of high importance for the man is known to create a balance and also known to create chaos.

        As mentioned before man was not built weak. So, why should a man settle for less. There is no greater wisdom which forbids a man to risk things for a greater good of mankind and self. There exists no greater failure than failing to try. A philanthropic approach to why a man should work for the greater good of self is to realise the fact that we live in a universe of connected consciousness where actions of every individual has a significant impact on others.


“O, Man what is it that you desire? “ asketh the universe . The man in his ignorance fails to answer. Closed his eyes , ears and locked his heart and threw the keys in the deepest and the darkest dungeons of ignorance.

It is time for Man to realise what he can achieve in his full potential for it is his dharma. As far as karma is concerned a man can only wait for the fruits of dharma as it is subject to market risks.
